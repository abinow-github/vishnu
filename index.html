Vishnu Ayurveda college was established in the year of 2002, located at Kulappully, Palakkad District, 4km away from the Shoranur railway station.It is situated in the premises of Vishnu Pharmaceuticals and Nursing home which was started in the year 1991.TheVishnu ayurveda college was made a reality by a great visionary Dr.P Ramachandran Nair. He is a renowned ayurveda practitioner and a great researcher.With a great futuristic vision, he is tirelessly working for acquiring high standards and quality of the organization. This college has been recognized by National Commission for Indian System of Medicine, under Ministry of AYUSH, Govt Of India.The college is affiliated to Kerala University of Health Sciences, Thrissur.Now Vishnu Ayurveda College is one among the best ayurveda colleges in India.

About Director

Dr.P.Ramachandran Nair had started his medical practice in 1965. Within a short span of time, he became popular as a physician in the vicinities of Kochi the Industrial Capital of Kerala. His lust for clinical practice and sincere quest for delivering the benefits of Ayurveda to needy patients of the society especially the downtrodden people made him wilfully forsake teaching job offered in the Gujarat Ayurveda University. With this vision he returned to Cochin and restarted his practice in 1968-69.

Dr M.Indiradevi

Proprietor

Our proprietor Dr. M. Indiradevi completed her BAMS from Govt. Ayurveda college Tripunithara Ernakulam in 1989. After which a hospital named Vishnu pharmaceuticals and nursing home was started near Shoranur in 1992, Which was further enhanced to Vishnu Ayurveda College in the year 2002, with a vision of fostering an institution which embrace the value of Ayurveda and to epitomize the quality of education in enriching the future generation of Ayurveda doctors.
